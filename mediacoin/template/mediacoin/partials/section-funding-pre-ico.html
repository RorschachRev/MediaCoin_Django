Why participate in MediaCoin now?

Limited Offer

All tokens issued during the fixed price will be offered at a special rate of 900 QMTC (MediaCoin Tokens) being equal to 1 ETC (Ethereum Classic). Once the fixed price period has ended, QMTC will be available, however it will have to be purchased at market value, and will no longer be married to the price of ETC, making the fixed price period the most exciting time to participate in MediaCoin.

Disruptive Technology

MediaCoin employs powerful encryption software when distributing the content in it's archives. This prevents the need for artists to restrict access to their content for fear that it will be illegally distributed. In short, we are creating a world in which artists can finally engage with their consumers in a trustless environment that not only provides the consumer unrestricted access to their published content immediately, but also ensures that the artists are fairly compensated for the content they are producing.

Useful and practical application

QMTC is the token that is used within the MediaCoin platform. It is the sole token that is accepted, and it alone allows access to the vast library of encrypted content within the MediaCoin catalog that is not available anywhere else legally, or illegally.

Technical Innovation

The team developing the MediaCoin Platform has decades of experience in developing complex applications, and this is just the start. MediaCoin has a multitude of projects, released in phases, that will undoubtedly revolutionize the world we live in. At its core, MediaCoin stands for honesty, integrity, and an unwavering dedication to security and innovation.

What is MediaCoin?

Mediacoin is one of the 1st platform for musicians and content creators to secure their work or content. Users will be able to publish video, audio and streaming media via military grade encryption methods. For example, if a user downloads a song from our network, then they will be able to download it but not open it. Similar to Bitcoin, they would need the keys for this particular file because the content would be locked using high encryption. It will also allow them to offer their content on MediaCoin's platform on a pay per play basis. We will also be moving into the live stream market and seeding market. MediaCoin is a platform for secure publishing, distribution, and payment. We are bringing the power back to the creator, artists, and authors of their content.

Legal Exclusions

Please read more about consumer rights at pleaseprotectconsumers.org if you value the privacy and secrecy of your private keys.
